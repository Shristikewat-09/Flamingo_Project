Greetings & Introductions

Learn how to greet people and introduce yourself confidently in English.

Common Greetings

  • Hello!
  • Good Morning!
  • Good Afternoon!
  • Good Evening!
  • Good Night!

Introducing Yourself

Example phrases:

  • My name is John.
  • I am from India.
  • It’s nice to meet you.

Asking Questions

  • What is your name?
  • Where are you from?
  • How are you?

Polite Expressions

  • Please
  • Thank you
  • You're welcome
  • Excuse me
  • Sorry

Practice: Fill in the blanks

Fill in the correct response:

  • ________! (Hint: Say hello)
  • My name ______ John.
  • It’s _______ to meet you.
