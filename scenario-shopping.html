Shopping Scenario

Learn how to shop confidently in English. This module covers key vocabulary, useful dialogues, and interactive exercises to master shopping conversations.

Key Vocabulary

  • Price: The cost of something.
  • Discount: A reduction in the price.
  • Bill/Receipt: A printed record of purchase.
  • Bargain: Negotiate the price.
  • Cashier: The person at the checkout counter.
